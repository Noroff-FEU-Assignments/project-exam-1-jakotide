About Us

Our story and vision

Welcome to GOOSE, the blog where three eccentric goose brothers take the music world by storm with their love for strange electronic devices and synthesizers from the future.

Prepare to dive into a world where honks meet harmonies, feathers meet frequencies, and beaks meet beats. Strap on your imagination goggles and get ready for a satirical journey unlike anything you've experienced before.

At GOOSE, we believe that music has the power to transcend species and genres.

Who says only humans can appreciate a good melody? Our goose brothers, Gustav, Gideon, and Gary, have dedicated their lives to unraveling the mysteries of music and testing out peculiar contraptions that even the most curious humans would consider utterly useless. But hey, what's life without a little quirkiness?

So, fellow wanderer of the auditory wilderness, get ready to embark on a journey that will challenge your eardrums, tickle your funny bone, and make you question the very fabric of musical reality. Welcome to GOOSE, where the geese are never afraid to spread their wings and let their weirdness soar.
